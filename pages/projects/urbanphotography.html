Urban Photography

Street photography

Me as a photographer


When I’m introducing myself it’s inevitable for me to talk about my passion for photography.

As an amateur photographer I feel a great connection with images and the idea of capturing a moment that will stay frozen in time.

With this project I attempt to capture the beauty of things through the lens of my own interpretation.

In this gallery you can find pictures surrounding different themes. Some showcasing the streets of Buenos Aires, some paintings or sculptures from museums or just simple moments or images that I reckoned was definitely worth capturing.

The gear I use: Sony Alpha 3500 with fix lens; super takumar 50mm 1.4, paximat automatic 28mm 2.8. Fujifilm X100.
